Rules

By registering for the Command & Conquest system, you agree to abide by the rules as follows:

  1. Players will not harass, intimidate, or abuse other players or moderators.
  2. Players will not send repeated, unsolicited notifications or interaction requests.
  3. Players will not act rudely towards other players, moderators, or Shard.
  4. Players will not use offensive, sexist, racist, or derogatory language, including in customizing their nation.
  5. Players will not intentionally disrupt gameplay or sabotage other players’ enjoyment of the game. This includes rage-quitting.
  6. Players will not attempt to enforce rules by their own action. Players will appeal to moderators to resolve any issues.
  7. Players will respect the spirit of the game. Players will abide by the decisions of the moderators.

Moderators are given the authority to utilize their moderation powers to enforce order, even if a player is not explicitly breaking a listed rule. No rule or policy of the Command & Conquest system shall be construed to supersede any rule or policy of the Thegye Discord server.
